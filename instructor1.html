Meet Our Instructors

Christopher Adam Bumstead (born 2 February 1995), also known as CBum, is a Canadian retired professional bodybuilder. Born in Ottawa, Ontario, he made his competitive debut in 2014 and earned his IFBB pro card in 2016. After placing second in the Mr. Olympia Classic Physique category in 2017 and 2018, he won six consecutive titles from 2019 to 2024, making him the most successful athlete in the category's history. Widely considered one of the greatest Classic Physique bodybuilders of all time, his influence has helped bring mainstream attention to bodybuilding globally.

Gurbani Judge (born 29 November 1987), better known as Bani J or VJ Bani, is an Indian fitness model, actress, and former MTV India presenter. She gained fame after participating in MTV Roadies 4, Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi 4, and Bigg Boss 10. She has also starred in the web series Four More Shots Please! and the film Hume Tumse Pyaar Kitna. A passionate fitness enthusiast, Bani J has been a brand ambassador for multiple fitness brands and continues to inspire many with her dedication to health and strength.
